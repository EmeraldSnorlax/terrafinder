How to Play

Click the get a new map button. Click on the map, or type the coordinates where you think the location is.
Click 'Guess' to recieve your score. A lower distance is better.
If you get a garbage map (i.e. one that doesn't load, or is just black, etc), feel free to just generate a new game. I can't really do much to filter them out in a resource efficient way.
